About On-Geo

What is On-Geo Manager?

On-Geo Manager connects people in an organisation and controls access to selected resources based on location. It helps communities manage updates, notices, and attendance from one place.

Location aware

Compare user location against active checkpoints before recording attendance.

Attendance first

Keep a daily register of people who are present within the accepted range.

Community updates

Share posts and notices with the active organisation or community.
